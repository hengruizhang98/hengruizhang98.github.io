About

Biography

Currently, I am a first year Ph.D. student(start from August 2020) at Computer Science department of University of Illinois at Chicago. I am advised by Prof. Philip S. Yu in the Big Data and Social Computing Lab .

Before joining UIC, I received B.Eng degree in Information Security from School of Electronics Information and Electrical Engineering of Shanghai Jiao Tong Univeristy

My research interests lie in data mining especially network mining, and their applications including social network analysis, recommender systems, etc.
